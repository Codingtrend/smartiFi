How to Find Your MAC Address

Windows: Open Command Prompt, type `ipconfig /all`, and look for the "Physical Address".

macOS: Go to System Preferences > Network > Advanced > Hardware. The MAC Address is listed there.

Android/iOS: Go to Settings > About Phone/Device > Status. The "Wi-Fi MAC address" will be visible.
